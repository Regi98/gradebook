Who We Are

We are a Private, Non-Profit, Non-Sectarian Learning Institution. Recognized by the Commission on Higher Education (CHED) and Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA).

The Jose C. Feliciano College Foundation all started with a man dreaming of providing the Filipino Youth the best venue and opportunity to realize their full potentials. He conceived of organizing a School of Science and Technology, knowing that education is for all and considered an integral requisite of human and community development. And, he succeeded. That small school which he started was the modest beginning of the College today.

MISSION

Jose C. Feliciano College Foundation will provide: holistic, globally responsive, relevant and quality education in all disciplines. With this mission, it aims to produce high-level professionals and leaders who are self-reliant, self sufficient, socially responsible and God-loving individuals.

VISION

Jose C. Feliciano College Foundation envisions itself to be the leading provider of holistic, responsive, relevant and globally recognized quality education that is based on the arts, sciences and information technology.

PHILOSOPHY

Jose C. Feliciano College Foundation believes that through quality education man can make a positive difference in the socio-economic development of the nation thereby uplifting the quality of life of every Filipino and contribute to the preservation of the resources in the national and international environment.

    Student Services

    The Office of Student Services and Affairs (OSSA) exist for the students. Its reason for being is to provide opportunities for growth, enhance the quality of life of student n school, complement the academic pursuit by providing various services and activities that shall contribute to the total development of the person, prepared to be an effective, competent and productive member of society.

    Services

  • Enforcement of the general regulations on Student Discipline
  • Coordination of Student Organizations and Activities
  • Management of the identification card services
  • Report and document cases of theft incident/complaint reports
  • Processing of Certificates of Good Moral Character
  • Issuance of forms for activities
  • Assist in the supervision of Community Extension Service activities
  • Issuance of request for the gate passes on uniform
  • Processing of scholarship application

    • General Policy Statement

    JCFC provides an opportunity for academically outstanding students to pursue their education through its academic scholarship program. The program is part of the school’s commitment and dedication to academic excellence and outstanding scholastic achievement. Furthermore, JCFC believes that students can strive for academic excellence through process of reward and recognition.

    Terms and Conditions

  • Applicants to the student assistantship must:
  • be bonafide students of the College.
  • have a minimum load of 15 academic units per semester.
  • belong to a family whose annual income does not exceed Php 100,000.00 annually.
  • have good academic standing (no grade below 80%).
  • be of good moral conduct and integrity.
  • be physically fit to work and with positive attitude towards JCFC.
  • pass the screening tests to be administered by the college.
  • pass the interview to be administered by the Dean/Head in coordination with the Dean of Office of Student Services and Affairs have parental consent.
  • have proven skills sufficient to meet the work requirements.
  • pass the semestral appraisal.
  • has not been charged and proven of violating any of the school policies.

    • Student Assistantship

    The Student Assistantship Program aims to achieve JCFC’s mission of making education available to all as highly expressed in its desire to promote social responsibility among the administrators, faculty, non-teaching staff and students. It is designed to provide opportunity to poor but deserving and intellectually capable students to pursue a college education by assigning them as student assistants in the various departments of the college. JCFC shall grant 100% basic tuition fee (excluding miscellaneous fees) scholarship to student assistants who will render minimum of (4) hours of service daily from Monday to Friday.

    Academic Achievement Scholarship

    Students with a general weighted average of at least 90% in academic performance including PE & NSTP for any particular semester of study and who shall have satisfied all the terms and conditions indicated herein shall be given an academic achievement scholarship based on the following:

    97 – above – 100% scholarship (President’s scholar)

    95 – 96 – 75% scholarship (Dean’s Lister)

    93 – 94 – 50% scholarship

    90 – 92 – 25% scholarship
